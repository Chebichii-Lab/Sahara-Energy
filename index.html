About Sahara Energy.

Sahara Energy Limited was incorporated in 2014 with the aim of being the energy provider of choice in the region, and ultimately, Africa. Our registered office and headquarters are in Nairobi, Kenya. Our core business is the importation, distribution and marketing of refined fuel products to all our markets in the region, performed by a team comprised of highly experienced professionals in the oil and gas space.

Mission Statement

To be the region’s energy company of choice by providing an unrivaled customer experience, while supporting our community and protecting the environment.

Vision Statement

To be the leading energy company in creating sustained value for our customers, employees, partners, and communities we operate in.

Core Values

Energy. Efficiency. Excellence.

Our Products

Sahara Energy’s range of products will allow us to keep you powered efficiently in the skies, on the roads and in every corner of East Africa through our dedicated stations.
